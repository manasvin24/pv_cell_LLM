SDG&E Considerations

Under current net billing rules, excess solar energy exported to the grid is credited at lower rates compared to older NEM policies.
This makes battery storage more financially attractive than before.

Time-of-use rates significantly impact solar payback period.
High evening peak rates increase value of stored solar energy.
